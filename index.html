🚫 Say No to Plastic!

🌍 Protect Our Planet, 🌊 Save Our Oceans

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest threats to our environment. It kills marine life, contaminates water, and takes centuries to decompose. Together, we can create a cleaner, greener world by saying no to plastic.

✅ What You Can Do:

  • 👜 Carry reusable bags
  • 🥤 Avoid single-use bottles & straws
  • 🍱 Choose eco-friendly packaging
  • 🔁 Recycle responsibly
  • 🌱 Support plastic-free initiatives

🌟 Together, we can make a difference! 🌟
